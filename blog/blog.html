Do drugs stimulate creativity?6/8/2017

Art has always been closely related to the use of drugs. Let’s imagine the “raw material” as something noisy and without control as if it were a tzigane orchestra in one of Kusturica’s movies. That orchestra (disguised as historical characters playing music with seafood and Pisa towers) is on the way leading to a small village. At the entrance of the village, a couple of policemen stop them and ask them to lower the noise, to get rid of their costumes and to stop playing music with seafood and Pisa towers. Does all of this make any sense? This is what happens when the material coming from the subconscious level goes through censorship before entering conscience. The “me” side of the personality rebuilds the distorted and ludic content with the help of control, order, logic and reason.
