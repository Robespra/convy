Front End Checklist

We made a list of the most common front-end oversights when launching so you can make sure your website is fully optimized.

1) Are all your CSS files are minified and loaded before the Concatenated Javascript in the HEAD ?

2) Are the 404 and 5xx pages existing ?

3) Have you tested your page with W3C validator and Google Page Speed Insight?

4) Did you run your page with adblockers enabled?

5) Is your page fully responsive ?

6) Bonus! (a list of 5 was not enough!)

Did you run a HTML Lint?, Checked all your links? , Provided a reset CSS?, Does your Page have a H1?, Are all the SEO stuff in place (analytics code installed, headings logic, sitemaps are available) ? ...
